About Us

Upcountry Lions Cricket Club is a premier cricket team that has made a remarkable mark in regional cricket. With a total of 50 matches played and an impressive 42 victories, the Lions have consistently demonstrated skill, strategy, and determination on the field. The team is known for its strong batting lineup, capable of setting high scores under pressure, and fast bowlers who can dominate any opposition with pace and precision. Beyond individual talent, the Lions are celebrated for their teamwork, discipline, and competitive spirit, which have earned them respect and admiration from fans and rivals alike. Every match is approached with a strategic mindset, combining experience, agility, and a love for the game. With a commitment to nurturing young talent and promoting cricket in the region, Upcountry Lions continues to be a symbol of sporting excellence and a source of inspiration for aspiring cricketers.
